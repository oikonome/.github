<h1 align="center">Oikonome</h1>

<p align="center"><b>Self-hosted personal &amp; family finance that emails you one honest answer every morning: are you on budget?</b></p>

---

Oikonome pulls your accounts, spending, recurring bills, and net worth into one
place and boils it down to a single plain email each day. The tool does the
checking so you don't have to open an app — and it all runs on hardware you own.

- **📬 The email is the product.** A daily verdict — on, over, or under — with
  the one number to hit today and the *why* behind it. No dashboard habit needed.
- **🔒 Your data, your hardware.** Runs on a box you own. No ads, no data
  selling, no telemetry, no lock-in. Categorization happens on-box via a local
  LLM, so nothing about your finances leaves your server.
- **🔁 Bring data your way.** Live feeds through Plaid, MX, or SimpleFIN;
  community API scripts; or CSV / OFX / QIF / PDF imports — all de-duplicated
  and reversible.
- **🛟 Multi-source & resilient.** Link two sources to one account: the
  healthiest serves the data, the rest stand by as automatic backups.
